Katapult is a minimal grid-based home screen launcher designed for the Mudita Kompakt, but works on any Android device running Android 12 or newer.

It gives you a clean black-and-white interface with a 3×4 app grid, a dock for Phone/SMS, and a music widget — nothing more than you need.

Setup

• Long-press any empty area of the home screen to open Settings
• Long-press the Phone, SMS, or extra dock shortcuts to replace them
• Long-press the clock or date to assign an app shortcut

Features

• 3×4 app grid with swipe or chevron pagination
• Music widget with playback controls — works with Auxio, Fossify Music, Spotify, Foobar, CalmCast, AntennaPod, and more
• Icon tinting and shaping for a uniform black-and-white look (works with ~95% of apps)
• Monochrome icons automatically used when the app provides one
• Custom icon import — pick a bundled Katapult icon or import your own PNG/SVG
• Icon shape: Circle or Rounded — affects all icons, buttons, and indicators
• Extra dock row with 3 additional shortcuts
• Notification indicators on app icons (requires notification listener permission)
• Clear notifications for a specific app from the app context menu
• Battery percentage with a level-based icon above the clock — tap to open battery settings
• AM/PM indicator for 12-hour clocks
• Hide status bar for a cleaner look
• E-Ink refresh on home: flashes the screen when pressing the home button to clear ghosting
• E-Ink mode cycle (Mudita Kompakt only): Disabled, Clear, Contrast, Reading
• Double-tap brightness: toggle display brightness by double-tapping the home background (requires Write Settings)
• Infinite scroll: wrap from the last page back to the first
• Simple wallpaper support (not managed by Android)
• Hide apps from the context menu or in bulk from the home menu
• Rename any app by long-pressing it in the grid
• App context menu: Reorder, Rename, Change Icon, Hide, App Info, Notifications, Clear Notifications, Uninstall
• Reorder apps with swap-based reordering, or reset back to alphabetical order

Permissions

• QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — list installed apps
• WRITE_SETTINGS — double-tap brightness toggle
• REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES — uninstall apps from the context menu
• BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE — read notifications for badge counts
• READ_CALL_LOG — missed call badge on Mudita Phone (Mudita Kompakt only)
• READ_SMS — unread SMS badge on Mudita Messages (Mudita Kompakt only)

Katapult is free, open source, and ad-free forever.
